Forget Me Nots

There are 1,924 emails and 14,TK chat messages of Allison’s ex-boyfriends in her inbox. Remnants of a relationships that tell partial stories of the beginning, middle and end of each relationship.

It’s the digital equivalent of the shoe box of memorabilia people sometimes keep in a dusty corner of their attic. Their content oscillates between humorous and logistical, profound and mundane.

This raw material is available to her to be interpreted and re-interpreted again and again. A keystroke or two and she can sieve through raw bits of conversations that took places weeks, months and even years ago.
